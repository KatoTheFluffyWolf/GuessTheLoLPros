How to play

Protect your 100-point score.

  1. Every hint shows its price before you unlock it.

  3. If status is revealed as active first, that entry is highlighted and costs 35 points.

How to play

Guess the daily pro player

The portrait is not a purchasable or unlockable hint. It remains hidden throughout the game and is revealed together with the player's name when the answer is correct.

Every daily run begins with 100 points. Nationality, date of birth, career status, First Stand, MSI, and Worlds each have their own price. Normal team history entries cost 20 points and unlock only that one row.

The latest team-history entry is always purchasable for 20 points while career status is unknown. That is a gamble: a retired player's final team may not be very useful. If status is revealed as active before purchase, its border is highlighted and its price becomes 35 points. Retired players keep the normal 20-point price.

Wrong guesses do not reduce your score. The challenge is to identify the player while keeping as many points as possible, and hints become unavailable when you cannot afford them.

This demo stores your attempts and unlocked hints in this browser so a refresh does not erase the current daily run.
